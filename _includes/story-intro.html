Xorro P2P is a mini BitTorrent peer-to-peer file sharing network built by a trio of developers.

Check out our individual websites:

  • Ken Chen
  • David Kurutz
  • Terry Lee

Due to an interest in P2P networks and distributed systems, we set out to build our own from scratch. The version available today is our alpha release, giving us a working proof-of-concept. We want to continue improving upon Xorro and we list upcoming features further down this page.

This site goes through our journey and will hopefully give readers a better understanding of what it takes to build your own P2P network.
